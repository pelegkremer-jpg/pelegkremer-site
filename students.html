Students

Current and former graduate and undergraduate researchers.

Working with students is one of the most rewarding parts of this job. Below are current advisees and alumni, with brief project descriptions. If you are a prospective student interested in spatial analysis of urban systems, please be in touch.

Current graduate students

  • Charles Wildonger – Heavy metal contamination and transport in green stormwater infrastructure.
  • Mac Sanders – Observational analysis of human activity, physical conditions, and social context around GSI sites in Kensington, Philadelphia.
  • Prottoy Roy – Spatiotemporal modeling of nuisance flood risk: a critical review of urban-scale approaches.
  • Emily Myers – Relating air pollution, social vulnerability and health outcomes in 500 US cities.
  • Sarah Scheller – Impacts of gentrification on garden land security.

Current undergraduate students

  • Anna Koisewroski – Honors thesis on redlining and the legacy of environmental degradation in Philadelphia and Pittsburgh.
  • Sophia Lemmons – Philadelphia Garden Data Collaborative.
